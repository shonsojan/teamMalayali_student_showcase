WELCOME TO
INDUSTRY NIGHT 2025

Discover the future of design and development as our graduating students from the Interactive Media Design program showcase their innovative portfolios, client projects, and creative talents. This event is a celebration of their hard work, collaboration, and skill, providing a platform to connect with industry professionals and explore the cutting-edge ideas shaping tomorrow's digital landscape. We invite you to explore the portfolios, meet the creators, and witness firsthand the potential of the next generation of media professionals.


Dive into a diverse range of work created by our talented students, from dynamic websites and interactive apps to creative media designs. Each portfolio reflects not only the technical skills but also the passion and creativity these students bring to the digital world. Whether you’re an industry professional looking for fresh talent or someone curious about the future of media design, this showcase offers a glimpse into what’s next in the field of interactive media. We’re excited to share their journey with you!

INTERACTIVE MEDIA DESIGN

The Interactive Media Design program at Fanshawe College is designed for aspiring creatives who are passionate about blending technology with design. Our curriculum equips students with a comprehensive skill set in areas such as web development, graphic design, user experience (UX), and multimedia production. Through hands-on projects, collaborative experiences, and exposure to industry-standard tools, students learn to create engaging, user-centered digital solutions. This program not only fosters technical proficiency but also encourages critical thinking and innovation, preparing graduates to thrive in the fast-paced world of interactive media.
